Transforming Lives Through Skill Development

Camp United offers youth ages 12-21 experiencing social, developmental, or emotional challenges a supportive and enriching 3 week summer program designed to enhance their life, develop positive relationships and develop practical skills.

Camp Features

Personal Development

Empowering participants to enhance their self-awareness, set meaningful goals, cultivate resilience, and build self-confidence.

Safe and Inclusive Environment

CU is dedicated to fostering diversity, equity, and inclusion among participants, staff, and volunteers.

Proficiency-Based Activities

Community Engagement

Our Founder

Sonia Barrett, BA CYW

With over 20 years of experience as a child and youth counselor, Sonia envisioned a nurturing environment where young individuals facing challenges could not only learn and grow but also experience joy and camaraderie. The journey of Camp United is rooted in Sonia's visionary leadership, driven by her unwavering passion for empowering youth and expanding access to support services for children and adolescents navigating social, developmental, or emotional hurdles. Through her dedication, she hopes this innovative youth initiative will become a beacon of hope and opportunity for countless young lives.
